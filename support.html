Frequently asked questions

Is Kcal free?

Kcal comes with a 7-day free trial. After that, a single one-time purchase unlocks the app forever. There are no subscriptions and no recurring fees.

I paid but the app is still locked. What do I do?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore purchases". This re-checks your purchase with Apple and unlocks the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to buy it. If it still doesn't unlock, email us and we'll help.

How do I log a food?

Tap "Log food" or the plus on any meal, then choose how you want to add it: scan a barcode, search by name, snap a photo for an AI estimate, describe your meal in words, or enter the values manually.

How does the AI estimate work?

When you use AI photo or AI describe, the photo or text you provide is sent to an AI service purely to estimate the calories and macros for that one entry. You can review and edit the result before saving. AI estimates are limited to 10 per day and are approximate — always adjust if you know the real values.

A barcode scan says "Product not found".

Not every product is in the food databases yet, especially regional items. When that happens, you can search for the food by name instead, or add it manually with the values from the label — and save it to your Food Library so it's there next time.

Where is my data stored?

Everything you log stays on your device. Kcal has no account and no server of its own, and it only contacts outside services when you actively look up a food. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual entries inside the app, or remove everything at once by deleting the app from your device. There is no account to close.

Do reminders work in the background?

Kcal schedules gentle meal reminders when you open the app. If you don't open it for a couple of days, reminders may pause until your next visit.
